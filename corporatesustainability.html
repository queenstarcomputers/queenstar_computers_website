CORPORATE SUSTAINABILITY

Generally, sustainability refers to the ability to maintain or support a process continuously over time.
In business and policy contexts, the major aim of sustainability is to prevent the depletion of physical
or natural resources, so that they will remain available for the long term.

At Queenstar Computers we recognize and appreciate the significance of corporate sustainability through
the pursuance of societal goals, particularly those relating to sustainable development i.e.,
environmental protection, social justice, equity, and economic development.

In undertaking our duties and offering services to our customers we always aim to
create long-term stakeholder value by implementing a business strategy whose major
focus is on the ethical, social, cultural, economic, and environmental dimensions.

This is in tandem with the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015.
At Queenstar Computers we recognize that ending poverty and other deprivations is highly linked with strategies that
improve health and education, promote equality, and facilitate economic growth while
at the same time preserving our environment to curb climate change menace.

3 Pillars of Corporate Sustainability

1) The Environmental Pillar: this is concerned with acting in an environmentally friendly manner through the reduction of waste and carbon footprint.

2) The Social Pillar: this is concerned with supporting the consumers, employees, and the general community associated with the business.

3) The Economic Pillar: this focuses on the financial aspect of the business. A business must be profitable to be sustainable.

How We Engage in Corporate Sustainability

Reducing carbon footprint

This is through the use of green appliances like laptops and rechargeable batteries. Also, adopting a paperless culture whereby very few documents are printed and a bulk of them are kept online.

Promoting Equality

Our company promotes equality in various ways such as the provision of an equitable working environment for employees regardless of their gender, religion, ethnicity, health conditions, etc.

Stakeholder Partnerships

We engage in worthwhile partnerships oriented toward sustainable development. We achieve this through the mobilization and sharing of expertise, technology, and financial resources.

Community support

We engage in corporate social responsibility through various platforms both face-to-face and online that may be present both at the local and national levels. For instance, drought support programs.
