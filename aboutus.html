Who We Are

Queenstar Computers was initiated in June 2010 in a rented apartment in Kasuku with a rich emphasis in Cyber services, software and hardware maintenance and supply, networking, and consultations in various Information and Communication Technology domain. We are a friendly IT company whose aim is to provide quality services and products to our clients through the use of modern computing technology. Today, the business has grown through reaps and bounds and has added other services such as Software Development, and Data Analysis.
Our Company aims to provide ICT based solutions to individuals, enterprises, government, and private and public organizations both locally and abroad.
Currently, the company supports a number of clients across the country. Our customers operate in a various sectors including retail, law, engineering, accounting, sales and marketing, banking, agriculture, religion, corporate, among others.
Our main office is located in Kasuku, Lesirko Building , First Floor, Room B3, along Kirimangai Road.
