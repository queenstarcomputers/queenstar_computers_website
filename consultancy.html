IT CONSULTANCY

IT consultancy includes advisory services that help clients assess different technology strategies and, in doing so,
use Information and Communication Technology (ICT) in the best way possible to achieve their business objectives.
By using ICT clients are able to align their technology strategies with their process and business strategies.
Our company advises our clients on a variety of ICT issues such as Cybersecurity, Disaster Recovery & Business continuity,
Cloud Infrastructure Management, End-User Services, Governance, Risk, & Compliance, ICT Strategy & Planning etc.

Cybersecurity

This entails application of processes, technologies and controls to protect computer systems, programs, networks, devices and data from cyber-attacks. The major aim is to reduce the risk of cyber-attacks and protect against the unauthorised exploitation of technologies, systems, and network.

Disaster Recovery Plan

This entails the procedures, processes and policies that prepare an organization’s vital IT infrastructure to recover from natural or human-induced disasters in an effective manner hence ensure business continuity. DRP enables companies to resume their normal operations in the shortest time possible after a disaster.

Cloud Infrastructure Management

This comprises the tools and processes required to allocate and deliver key resources when efficiently and where they are required. The major benefit of incorporating cloud infrastructure management into your business is that there is minimal hardware maintenance or up-gradation required.

ICT Project Management

This is the process of planning, organizing and allocating responsibility for the completion of specific information technology (ICT) goals of an organization. This normally includes engaging in projects to scale ICT processes, introduce new software solutions, or switch cloud providers for an organization.
