Our Privacy Policy

Any information we collect from users is very crucial as it enables us to personalize and improve our services.
This document gives a description of the types of information we collect from our users and how we use it.

Personal information you give us

We receive and store any information you provide to us on our website through subscription or provide to us in any other way.
Some types of Personal Information collected may include your name, e-mail address and
phone numbers that are entered on this website, sent to us by email, or through calling.

E-mail communications

The communications we receive through emails you send to us help us to improve our services hence meet customer’s requirements.

Personal information collected automatically

Just like other websites, log files are stored on the web server saving details like the visitor’s browser type, IP address, referring page and time of visit.
We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with this website.
We automatically receive and record information from your browser including
unique device identifiers, and mobile network information such as phone number, type of browser, IP address and the page you requested.
This information is collected and stored using Google Analytics.
Google Analytics is a web analytics service that provides statistics and basic analytical tools for Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and marketing purposes.

Google Ads

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies and analytics to serve ads.
Google uses the DART cookie to serve ads to visitors based on their visit to sites they visit on the Internet.
Google Analytics helps to track website performance and collect visitor insights.
It can help companies and businesses to determine top sources of user traffic, measure the success of their
marketing activities, discover patterns and trends in user engagement,
track goal completions (such as purchases, adding products to shopping carts), and obtain other visitor information like demographics.
However, Google may use the data gathered to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network.
However, website visitors may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

Browser cookies

Cookies are small pieces of text that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser
and tell us how and when pages in our site are visited and by how many people. Also, cookies help that website remember information about your visit,
which can both make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.
However, we do not mix the site usage information collected through cookies with other Personal Information.

Ads that may appear on this website may be delivered to users by our advertising partners, who may set cookies.
Such cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer every time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or other users of your computer.
This information allows ad networks deliver promotional messages to a customer based on their specific traits, preferences, and interests, among other things.
Nevertheless, this Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by this website but do not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers or by Google Analytics.

How the Information is used

Provision, maintenance, protection and improvement of our service.

To customize the content, we offer to you such as giving you more relevant search results and adverts.

We use email address to send you newsletters and informing you about our services, offers by us or our affiliates.

For instance, we can e-mail you to inform you about upcoming changes or improvements. E-mail addresses will not be sold, leased or rented to 3rd parties.

We use information collected from cookies and other information collection technologies to improve your user experience and the overall quality of our services.

However, we will request for your consent before using information for a purpose other than those set out in this Privacy Policy.
We will also use information as required by law.

Visitor Options

If you have subscribed to one of our services, you may unsubscribe as you wish by following the instructions which are included in e-mail that you receive.
Also, you may be able to block cookies via your browser settings but this may prevent you from access to certain features of the website.
