Computer Networking

Networking refers to interconnected computing devices(such as desktops, laptops, tablets, servers, smartphones, etc.)
that can exchange data and share resources with each other.
These networked devices use a system of rules, called communications protocols, to transmit information over physical or wireless technologies.
It also entails interconnection of IoT devices (such as cameras, door locks, doorbells, audio/visual systems, refrigerators, thermostats, sensors, etc.)
that communicate with each other.
Our Company ensures that your networking requirements are taken care of thus enabling you to share resources optimally.

Benefits of Networks in a Business Organization

Resource Sharing

Networked access help to avoid data redundancy thus saving time and avoiding errors.

Minimizing Cost

This is achieved by storing information in a centralised database, and less IT support requirements.

Enhancing Business Efficiency

Users can share resources such as data, printers, scanners and copiers over a network.

Improving communication

Users can easily share data or access it from remote locations if it is kept on a server.
